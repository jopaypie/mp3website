Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful curse known as Ryomen Sukuna.

Black Clover

Black Clover follows Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything, who aims to become the Wizard King.

Kimetsu no Yaiba

Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister, Nezuko, who was turned into a demon.

Bungou Stray Dogs

Bungou Stray Dogs follows a group of detectives with supernatural powers who solve cases involving other individuals with extraordinary abilities.

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is about Ken Kaneki, a college student who becomes a half-ghoul after an encounter with one. He must navigate his new life while hiding his identity from both humans and ghouls.

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity lives inside enormous walled cities to protect themselves from the Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures who devour humans seemingly without reason.

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Assassination Classroom

Assassination Classroom follows a class of misfits who are trying to kill their new teacher, an alien octopus with bizarre powers and super strength. The teacher has just destroyed the moon and plans to destroy the Earth unless his students can destroy him first.

Kakegurui

Kakegurui is set at Hyakkaou Private Academy, where the student hierarchy is determined by gambling. Yumeko Jabami, a new student, turns the hierarchy on its head with her compulsive gambling style.

Horimiya

Horimiya follows the relationship between Kyoko Hori, a bright and popular high school girl, and Izumi Miyamura, a quiet and gloomy boy with a hidden side.

Kaguya-sama: Love is War

Kaguya-sama: Love is War follows Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, two high school geniuses who are too proud to confess their love and scheme to make the other one confess first.

Spy x Family

Spy x Family follows a master spy codenamed "Twilight" who is tasked with building a family to execute a mission, not knowing that the girl he adopts as his daughter is a telepath, and the woman he agrees to marry is a skilled assassin.

Haikyuu

Haikyuu!! is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. It follows the story of Shoyo Hinata, a volleyball enthusiast who dreams of becoming the best volleyball player despite his short height.
